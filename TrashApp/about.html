About the application

Application created as part of the
Hack Heroes 2019 competition.
Developers: Jakub Cywiński

We are a 3-person team from LO16 in Szczecin. We are taking part in the nationwide #HACKHEROES 2019. Our goal is to provide you with an application that will make you more ecological and learn something new about what lands in your trash. We hope you will use our website and be ECO!
